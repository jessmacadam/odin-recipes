FRIED EGGS

Overview:

A perfectly cooked fried egg can upgrade practically anything, from avocado toast to a double cheeseburger and beyond. If you’re looking to start making diner-worthy eggs right at home, we’re here to show you how to make perfect fried eggs, whether you prefer yours over-easy, sunny-side up, medium-hard, or over-hard. Follow our top tips below, and you’ll be slingin' eggs like a pro in no time.

Ingredients:

  • Lovely frying pan
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, chilli flakes
  • EGGS!!!!

For sunny side up:

  1. Heat olive oil in pan for 2-3 mins
  2. Crack egg carefully into pan, taking care not to split yolk
  3. Fry on medium heat until whites are cooked through, sprinkle with salt, pepper & chilli flakes
  4. Slide onto your toast or rice or hash browns. Eat with ketchup.

For fully fried eggs:

  1. Heat oil in a pan until very hot
  2. Carefully crack egg in pan
  3. Sprinkle with chilli flakes and a pinch of salt and pepper
  4. Keep heat on high until edges are crispy, then flip over for another minute with heat on low
  5. Serve and eat while hot with a lovely bit of toast
