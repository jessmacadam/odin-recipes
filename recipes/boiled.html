Overview:

How to boil eggs â€“ Bring water to a boil first, add eggs, start the timer. 6 minutes for runny yolks, 8 minutes for soft boiled (my go-to!), 10 minutes for hard boiled. Peel under water to make life easier.

Ingredients:

  • Nice big pan
  • Loads of eggs
  • Kettle
  • Water

For hard boiled eggs:

  1. Boil the water in the kettle
  2. Add salt
  3. Leave eggs for 11 minutes
  4. Transfer to cold or iced water
  5. Peel! And serve!

For soft boiled eggs:

  1. Boil the water in the kettle
  2. Add salt
  3. Leave eggs for 6-8 minutes
  4. Transfer to cold or iced water
  5. Peel! And serve!
